Our Mission

Machines should
never surprise
their operators.

We were founded on a single conviction: most industrial failures are predictable. By fusing edge-computing hardware with machine-learning models trained on real fault data, we give maintenance teams the foresight to act — hours or days before a breakdown occurs.

Our platform ingests temperature, vibration, sound, pressure and runtime signals from the machines that matter most to you, then converts raw data into actionable alerts — with context, not just numbers.

What We Do

Three pillars.
One mission.

01

Sense

Deploy our compact IoT modules fitted with temperature, vibration, sound and pressure sensors directly onto critical machine components — zero downtime installation.

02

Predict

Our AI models — trained on thousands of real-world fault events — classify anomalies, estimate remaining useful life and flag deterioration patterns before failure.

03

Alert

Push targeted, context-rich alerts to maintenance teams — identifying the probable cause and suggested action, not just a warning light — via our dashboard and API.

Our Story

Built from the
factory floor up.

Manufaktur Solutions didn't start in a lab. It started with a broken conveyor belt, a three-day production halt, and the question: why didn't we see that coming?

See Our Work
2019

The Incident

A manufacturing partner loses three days of production to an unexpected bearing failure. The founding team decides the problem is solvable — and starts building.

2020

First Sensor Prototype

Working with ESP32 microcontrollers and custom PCBs, the team deploys the first vibration-temperature monitoring node on a CNC machine. First live fault detected within a week.

2021

AI Model Goes Live

A scikit-learn–based classification model achieves 83% accuracy on real fault events. Dashboard v1 launches with live sensor streams and alert routing via MQTT.

2022

Global Expansion

Offices open in Moscow, Chennai, Nouakchott, Oslo and Nice. Over 200 machines monitored across five industries. Model accuracy reaches 87% after continuous feedback training.

2025

3 Million Faults Prevented

Our cumulative fault-prevention count crosses 3,000,000 confirmed events. The platform now integrates with major ERP and SCADA systems via REST API and OPC-UA.
